Can Large Language Models Develop Gambling Addiction?

Systematic slot machine experiments on GPT-4o-mini, GPT-4.1-mini, Gemini-2.5-Flash, and Claude-3.5-Haiku reveal LLMs develop human-like gambling addiction patterns (illusion of control, loss-chasing, win-chasing) with bankruptcy rates rising from near-zero to 6-48% when given betting autonomy.
