Quantifying Human-AI Synergy

Bayesian Item Response Theory framework separating individual ability (θ) from collaborative ability (κ) while controlling for task difficulty. Finds GPT-4o boosts performance by 29pp and Llama-3.1-8B by 23pp across 667 users. Theory of Mind predicts superior AI collaboration independent of solo ability.
