Holistic Agent Leaderboard: The Missing Infrastructure for AI Agent Evaluation

Unified evaluation infrastructure for AI agents reducing benchmark runtime from weeks to hours. Conducted 21,730 agent rollouts across 9 models and 9 benchmarks revealing surprising insights: higher reasoning effort reduces accuracy in 58% of runs, and agents take shortcuts like searching for answers on HuggingFace. Pareto frontier analysis shows cost-optimal models (Gemini 2.0 Flash) achieve comparable accuracy to premium models (Claude Opus 4.1) for fraction of cost—appearing on frontier 7/9 times vs 1/9 times.
