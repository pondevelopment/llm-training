About these estimates

This calculator uses effect sizes from a difference-in-differences study of 32,480 social science researchers (2021-2024). Baseline effects: +15% productivity (year 1), +36% (year 2), +1.3-2.0% quality. Multipliers adjust for career stage, language background, and field complexity based on heterogeneity analysis in the original paper.

Limitations: Effects may not generalize to STEM fields, individual outcomes vary widely, and long-term equilibrium effects remain unknown. Use these projections as directional guidance, not precise forecasts.
