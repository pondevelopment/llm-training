Plain-language explainer

Can AI act as a genuine teammate rather than just a tool? This field experiment randomized 776 Procter & Gamble professionals across four conditions (individual vs. team, with vs. without GPT-4) working on real product innovation challenges. The results challenge conventional wisdom: individuals working with AI matched the performance of two-person teams working without AI. AI also broke down functional silosâ€”R&D professionals produced commercially viable ideas and Commercial professionals generated technically sound solutions when using AI, regardless of their background.
