Why intertwined boundary work matters

Isolated defensive moves fail because they don't create expansion opportunities or enable necessary collaborations. The interdependencies (opportunity ties + necessity ties + recursive reinforcement) transform AI disruption from existential threat into strategic advantage. Organizations that map these cascading boundary shifts before deployment adapt successfully; those attempting isolated interventions struggle.
