Understanding the prediction

This simulator uses the linear regression coefficients from the study (n=1,016 university students) to predict educational use of Gen-AI on a 1-5 scale. The baseline is 3.0 (neutral), and each personality trait adds or subtracts based on its standardized coefficient (β).

Overall model coefficients:

Conscientiousness: β=0.267 (p<0.001)

Openness: β=0.250 (p<0.001)

Extraversion: β=0.117 (p=0.008)

Neuroticism: β=-0.127 (p=0.001)

Agreeableness: β=0.033 (p=0.436, n.s.)

Gender-specific differences:

Women: Conscientiousness β=0.285, Openness β=0.250, Extraversion β=0.134

Men: Conscientiousness β=0.273, Openness β=0.276, Extraversion β=0.087

Model explains 26.4% of variance (R²=0.264)
