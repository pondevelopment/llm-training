Understanding the prediction

This simulator uses the linear regression coefficients from the study (n=1,016 university students) to predict educational use of Gen-AI on a 1-5 scale. The baseline is 3.0 (neutral), and each personality trait adds or subtracts based on its standardized coefficient (β).
