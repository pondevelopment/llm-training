How the decline happens

Why hiring drives the effect (not layoffs):

The paper shows separations actually decreased slightly in adopting firms—retention improved, not worsened. The entire −9% junior employment decline comes from slower hiring: firms post fewer entry-level jobs as GenAI handles routine tasks. This "aging in place" pattern suggests firms expect permanent automation of junior work rather than temporary adjustments.
