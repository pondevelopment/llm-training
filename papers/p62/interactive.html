How to choose

  • Catastrophic: You’re in a high-stakes domain where a single bad answer can cause serious harm (health, money, legal, safety). Default to stricter gating and incident-style review.
  • Chronic: You expect lots of everyday usage where small errors accumulate (support, content ops). Default to telemetry/sampling and trend monitoring.
  • Balanced: You need both: catch rare severe events and track the everyday error rate. Default to two dashboards (incidents + prevalence).

Note

This interactive is a learning aid that summarizes the paper’s taxonomy and mitigation themes. It’s not an exhaustive catalog and it doesn’t replace a domain-specific safety review.
