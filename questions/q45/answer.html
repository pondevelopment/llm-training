🛠️ Core idea

Bias/error mitigation is layered. Surface controls (prompts, filters), data remediation & retrieval, then alignment tuning (SFT + RLHF/DPO) combine for durable reduction.

$$ r_{\text{after}} = r_0 \cdot \prod_{i=1}^{n} (1 - e_i), \quad 0 \le e_i \le 1 $$
  • r_0: base (pre-mitigation) risk
  • e_i: effectiveness of strategy i
  • r_{after}: residual after layering
  • Higher severity ↓ marginal returns
🎯 Prompting & guardrails
  • Clear refusals & structure
  • Self-check / critique passes
  • Safety & moderation filters
🧱 Data & retrieval
  • Balanced & counterfactual data
  • RAG factual grounding
  • Debias sampling / reweighting
🧪 Fine-tuning & RLHF
  • SFT curated policy data
  • DPO / RLHF preference shaping
  • Adversarial robustness training

🧭 Key levers

  • Map harms by severity × likelihood, then assign mitigation layers.
  • Track coverage: data diversity, retrieval recall, policy evaluation hits.
  • Run structured red-teaming to validate high-risk slices.

🎯 Why this matters

  • Fairness — reduce stereotype harm.
  • Reliability — keep answers factual.
  • Compliance — maintain audit readiness.
  • Trust — transparent controls for stakeholders.
