Core challenges

Shipping LLM features is more than "does the model sound good?" Teams juggle cost, latency, safety, interpretability, and privacy while still hitting product goals, and a weakness in any dimension can sink the launch.

  • Resource intensity: Serving GPUs, memory, and scaling strategy dominate spend.
  • Bias & safety: Training data quirks or poor guardrails can surface harmful outputs.
  • Interpretability: Deep models are hard to audit without strong tracing and evals.
  • Privacy & compliance: Data residency, retention, and regulatory controls must hold.
Performance
  • Latency targets (SLA) and throughput
  • Quantisation, batching, KV caching
Safety & quality
  • Instruction tuning, RLHF, guardrails
  • Evaluation loop & red teaming
Privacy & compliance
  • Pseudonymisation, on-prem/edge options
  • Auditability and data retention

Why this matters

  • Production readiness: Meet SLAs without runaway infra costs.
  • Trust & safety: Reduce bias, toxic completions, and policy violations.
  • Governance: Pass privacy, security, and audit checkpoints.
